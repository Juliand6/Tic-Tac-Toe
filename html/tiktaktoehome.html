Rules:

Tic-Tac-Toe is a 2 player game in which both players take turns placing their corresponding game pieces in a 3 by 3 grid. The game ends and a player wins when either player manages to connect 3 of their game pieces in a row. A row can be defined as any of the conjugations of the following spaces on the grid: 123, 147, 258, 369, 456, 789, 159, 357 (refer to picture on the left). At the start of the game both players will be randomly assigned a symbol, either Os or Xs, this will be your corresponding symbol for the game. Player X will always make the first move, and player O will make the second.
