> WELCOME TO TIC_TAC_TOE

Tic-Tac-Toe is a 2 player game in which both players take turns placing their corresponding game pieces in a 3 by 3 grid. At the start of the game both players will be randomly assigned a symbol, either Os or Xs, this willbe your corresponding symbol for the game (note that player X will always make the first move). The game ends and a player wins when either player manages to connect 3 of their game pieces in line either vertically, horizontally or diagonally along the grid.
